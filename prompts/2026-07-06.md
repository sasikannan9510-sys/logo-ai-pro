# Company Name

[BRAND NAME] (e.g., Veridian Labs)

# Industry

Premium Consumer Goods / High-End Lifestyle Brand (e.g., Luxury Skincare, Sophisticated Wellness, Artisanal Spirits) - focused on products requiring exquisite packaging and brand presentation.

# Website Purpose

To establish [BRAND NAME]'s premium market positioning, elegantly showcase its product line, communicate its core values and unique selling propositions, and drive brand engagement and conversion (e.g., product discovery, purchase intent, newsletter sign-ups). The website serves as a sophisticated digital flagship store and brand narrative platform.

# Target Audience

Discerning consumers who value quality, design, efficacy, and exclusivity. Individuals with an appreciation for minimalist aesthetics, premium materials, sustainable practices (if applicable), and a luxurious brand experience. This includes affluent individuals, design enthusiasts, and those seeking high-performance or meticulously crafted products.

# Brand Personality

Luxury, Clean, Modern, Elevated, Refined, Sophisticated, Authentic, Trustworthy, Articulate, Serene.

# Design Direction

An architectural, modern, and timeless aesthetic emphasizing spaciousness, pristine typography, and a refined editorial layout. The design should evoke a sense of exclusivity and quiet confidence, aligning with a premium, high-end brand. It's about presenting information with clarity and elegance, allowing content and product imagery to breathe and speak for themselves without visual clutter. The overall impression should be one of understated luxury and intelligent design.

# Visual Identity

Minimalist and highly polished. The visual identity will be characterized by generous white space, strong typographic hierarchy, high-quality and evocative imagery, and subtle interactive elements. The focus is on precision, clean lines, and a harmonious balance between visual elements, creating an immersive and premium user experience.

# Color Palette

*   **Primary Accent:** `#3A4B5C` (Deep Charcoal Blue) - For primary text, crucial elements.
*   **Secondary Accent:** `#7D8D9D` (Muted Slate Grey) - For secondary text, subtle accents.
*   **Primary Neutral:** `#FFFFFF` (Pure White) - Dominant background color, expansive white space.
*   **Secondary Neutral:** `#F9FAFC` (Off-White / Light Grey) - For subtle background variations, card backgrounds.
*   **Highlight/CTA:** `#C4A07F` (Warm Gold/Bronze) - For calls-to-action, key interactive states, subtle brand accents.
*   **Feedback/System:** A subtle green for success, and soft red for errors, consistent with the refined palette.

# Typography

A sophisticated pairing of a contemporary sans-serif for primary headings and body text, complemented by a refined serif or a distinct display sans-serif for high-impact headlines, quotes, or specific branding elements.

*   **Headings (Primary):** `Neue Haas Grotesk Display` (or a similar premium, clean sans-serif like `Inter` or `GT America`). Characterized by excellent legibility, subtle geometric precision, and a modern feel. Used for large titles, section headers.
*   **Body Text & UI:** `Inter` (or `IBM Plex Sans`, `Satoshi`). A highly legible, versatile sans-serif optimized for screens, ensuring clarity even at smaller sizes. Used for paragraphs, UI labels, navigation.
*   **Accent/Display (Optional):** `Canela Text` (or `Lora`, `Playfair Display`). A modern serif with elegant details for hero statements, pull quotes, or unique branding moments, providing a touch of classic sophistication.

# Layout Structure

The layout adheres strictly to a **Swiss Grid** and **Editorial Layout** principles, emphasizing order, structure, and hierarchy.

*   **Header / Navigation:**
    *   Minimalist, fixed (on scroll) header with a subtle scroll-down effect.
    *   Prominent brand logo (wordmark preferred) on the left.
    *   Clean, spacious primary navigation links (e.g., "Shop," "Our Story," "Science," "Journal," "Contact") on the right, or centrally aligned with subtle interaction states.
    *   Optional utility icons (e.g., Search, Cart/Bag, Account) discreetly integrated.
    *   Ample padding and vertical rhythm.

*   **Hero Section:**
    *   Full-width, high-resolution premium imagery or video that conveys the brand's essence and product experience (e.g., a serene product in an aspirational setting, elegant lifestyle shot).
    *   A concise, impactful headline in a large, elegant typeface, positioned thoughtfully within the grid, often off-center.
    *   A brief, compelling sub-headline.
    *   A singular, clear Call-to-Action button, elegantly styled, positioned to guide the user without overwhelming the visual.
    *   Generous white space surrounding content.

*   **Feature Sections:**
    *   Modular grid layouts (2-column, 3-column) for showcasing product categories, key benefits, or brand pillars.
    *   Each feature block contains high-quality imagery/icons, a concise title, and a short description.
    *   Emphasis on asymmetrical balance, staggered layouts, and vertical rhythm for visual interest while maintaining order.
    *   Subtle animations on scroll to reveal content.

*   **Content Sections:**
    *   Dedicated areas for brand storytelling, scientific backing, or ingredient highlights.
    *   Often featuring large, evocative imagery paired with meticulously typeset paragraphs.
    *   Editorial feel with pull quotes, clear subheadings, and ample line height and letter spacing for readability.
    *   Could include an "Our Story" or "Brand Philosophy" section with a blend of text and imagery.

*   **Call-to-Action (Integrated & Dedicated):**
    *   Strategic placement throughout the page.
    *   Both primary (e.g., "Shop Now," "Discover More") and secondary (e.g., "Learn More," "Sign Up") buttons.
    *   Dedicated CTA sections at strategic points, often featuring a distinct background (subtle texture or color) and a strong, concise message encouraging conversion.
    *   Always clear, concise, and visually distinct but integrated into the overall aesthetic.

*   **Footer:**
    *   Minimalist yet comprehensive.
    *   Typically a dark background (e.g., Deep Charcoal Blue) with light text for contrast.
    *   Includes essential links (Company, Support, Legal), social media icons, and a newsletter sign-up.
    *   Brand logo re-iteration and copyright information.
    *   Clean, well-organized columns with generous spacing.

# Grid System

A robust **8-point grid system** for all spacing and sizing, ensuring pixel-perfect alignment and consistent vertical and horizontal rhythm. The layout should leverage a **12-column flexible grid** for content distribution, with significant gutter widths to enhance the sense of space and structure. The Swiss Grid principles will dictate careful alignment of elements to a baseline grid.

# Spacing System

A modular **8-point spacing scale** (`8px, 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px, 64px, 80px, 96px, 128px, 160px...`) will be applied consistently for padding, margins, and gaps between components. Large white space is paramount, creating breathing room around elements and contributing to the premium, minimalist aesthetic. Vertical spacing between sections should be generous, often `128px` to `160px` or more.

# Components

All components should reflect the brand's premium, clean, and modern identity.

*   **Buttons:**
    *   **Primary:** Solid background (Highlight/CTA color) with white text, subtle hover effect (e.g., slight lift, subtle color change, or border reveal). Rounded corners (small radius, e.g., 4px).
    *   **Secondary:** Outline style (Deep Charcoal Blue border, transparent background) with Deep Charcoal Blue text, subtle fill on hover.
    *   **Tertiary/Text:** Pure text link with an underline on hover.
    *   All buttons have consistent padding, typography, and clear, concise labels.

*   **Cards:**
    *   Minimalist design with generous internal padding.
    *   Subtle background (Off-White/Light Grey) or pure white.
    *   Crisp, thin border (1px light grey) or very subtle shadow for depth, avoiding heavy drop shadows.
    *   Clear hierarchy of text and imagery within.
    *   Interactive cards will have a subtle hover state (e.g., shadow increase, border highlight).

*   **Forms:**
    *   Clean, un-styled input fields by default, with only a subtle bottom border or thin outline.
    *   Focus state: Clear highlight (e.g., a thin primary accent color border).
    *   Typography within fields matches body text.
    *   Error states clearly but subtly indicated (e.g., a light red bottom border).
    *   Labels are placed above fields for clarity, using the secondary accent color.

*   **Icons:**
    *   Minimalist, single-weight line icons (outline style).
    *   Consistent stroke weight and size.
    *   Monochromatic (Deep Charcoal Blue or white on dark backgrounds).
    *   Subtle hover effects for interactive icons.

*   **Dividers:**
    *   Thin, almost invisible horizontal lines (1px light grey or even transparent spacing blocks) used sparingly to separate content sections or elements, reinforcing the grid.
    *   Not always a literal line, often implied by white space.

# Imagery Style

The imagery should be high-quality, aspirational, and evocative, designed to tell a story and reinforce the brand's premium nature.

*   **Photography:**
    *   Clean, sophisticated, and editorial.
    *   Focus on natural light, serene environments, and authentic moments.
    *   Product photography should be studio-quality, showcasing the packaging and product details in a clean, elevated setting (similar to the packaging mockups described in the client brief).
    *   Lifestyle photography should be aspirational, featuring diverse individuals interacting with the product in a subtle, elegant manner.
    *   Limited color palette in photos, often desaturated or with specific color grading to match brand mood.
    *   Minimal distractions, strong composition.

*   **Abstract Visual Style (Optional):**
    *   Subtle, sophisticated textures or gradients used as background elements, reminiscent of premium materials (e.g., brushed metal, soft matte surfaces, delicate organic patterns).
    *   Architectural details, clean geometric forms, and subtle light play can be incorporated as abstract graphic elements if relevant to the brand story.
    *   Avoid literal illustrations unless they are highly stylized and align with a sophisticated, minimalist art direction.

# Interaction & Animation

All interactions should be subtle, swift, and purposeful, enhancing the user experience without being distracting or frivolous.

*   **Subtle Hover Effects:**
    *   Buttons: Slight background color shift, gentle border reveal, or a subtle "push" animation.
    *   Cards/Images: Slight scale up, subtle shadow increase, or a soft overlay reveal.
    *   Navigation Links: Underline reveal, color change, or subtle glow.
*   **Transitions:**
    *   Page transitions should be smooth and fast (e.g., quick fade-in/out).
    *   Accordion or dropdown animations should be fluid and elegant.
*   **Micro-interactions:**
    *   Form field focus states provide clear feedback.
    *   Loading spinners (if needed) should be minimal and refined.
    *   Scroll-based animations for revealing content blocks (e.g., slight fade-in, gentle slide-up) should be timed to enhance flow, not interrupt it.
    *   Parallax scrolling for hero imagery, used very subtly to add depth.

# Mobile Responsive Design

The desktop layout will gracefully adapt to mobile devices, prioritizing readability, navigability, and touch-friendliness.

*   **Content Stack:** Columns will generally stack vertically. Larger typefaces will scale down appropriately while maintaining hierarchy.
*   **Navigation:** A clear hamburger menu icon will replace the desktop navigation, revealing an overlay or slide-out menu. Essential utility icons (Search, Cart) remain accessible.
*   **Hero Section:** Image/video adjusts to fit the viewport; headline and CTA remain prominent but scaled.
*   **Spacing:** White space is preserved but adjusted (reduced) to prevent excessive scrolling on smaller screens. Padding and margins adapt for optimal readability.
*   **Imagery:** Images scale down, and potentially change aspect ratio slightly to optimize for mobile viewports without losing impact.
*   **Touch Targets:** All interactive elements (buttons, links) will have sufficiently large touch target areas (minimum 44x44px) for ease of use.
*   **Performance:** Mobile optimization for fast loading times is critical, with optimized image sizes and efficient code.

**Emphasize:** Architectural precision, strong visual hierarchy, extensive use of white space, crisp lines, geometric balance, refined material textures suggested through subtle gradients or shadows, and an overall sense of calm sophistication and high value. The aesthetic should be akin to a luxury art gallery or a high-end architectural portfolio.

# Negative Prompt

Avoid generic SaaS landing pages, AI robots, cryptocurrency style, neon gradients, marketing agency layouts, dashboard-heavy interfaces, excessive glassmorphism, busy backgrounds, cluttered layouts, stock AI imagery, cartoon illustrations, low-quality typography, distracting animations, loud colors, cheap aesthetics, and any visual clutter. Ensure no overly decorative elements or gratuitous UI gradients. No elements resembling typical e-commerce templates that lack unique brand identity. Ensure the design feels bespoke and custom, not templated.
