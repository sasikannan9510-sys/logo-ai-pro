# Company Name
Modern Coffee House

# Industry
Premium Coffee Shop / Cafe

# Website Purpose
To establish a sophisticated and inviting online presence that reflects the premium, modern, and minimalist brand identity of Modern Coffee House. The website will serve to:
1.  **Showcase:** Present the unique coffee offerings, artisanal products, and the inviting ambiance of the coffee house.
2.  **Inform:** Provide essential information such as menu, locations, operating hours, and brand story.
3.  **Engage:** Encourage visitors to explore the brand, learn about coffee origins, and appreciate the craft.
4.  **Drive Traffic:** Motivate in-store visits through compelling visuals and clear calls to action for locations.
5.  **Facilitate:** Potentially integrate future functionalities like online ordering, table reservations, or event bookings (though not primary for this homepage prompt).

# Target Audience
Discerning coffee enthusiasts, urban professionals, creatives, and individuals aged 25-55 who appreciate high-quality artisanal products, minimalist aesthetics, and a premium, curated experience. They value design, authenticity, and a serene atmosphere, often seeking a refined escape or a productive workspace.

# Brand Personality
Sophisticated, Refined, Authentic, Artisanal, Warm, Inviting, Minimalist, Premium, Timeless.

# Design Direction
The design direction is **Modern Minimalist** with a strong emphasis on **Premium, Architectural, and Editorial** aesthetics. It will be **Typography-driven**, utilizing a **Swiss Grid** for impeccable structure and **large white space** to create an elevated sense of calm and focus. The overall feel should be **timeless, clean, and highly professional**, reflecting the quality and craft of Modern Coffee House. The visual language will be **minimal and refined**, allowing content to breathe and shine without distraction.

# Visual Identity
Clean, geometric, and sophisticated. The primary visual element will be a stylized, flat vector coffee cup icon, representing simplicity and quality. This icon, alongside the brand name, will form a minimalist logo. Visuals will lean towards high-quality, authentic photography (or sophisticated abstract compositions) blended with subtle, flat vector illustrations to maintain the minimalist aesthetic. Consistency in line weight and style for all icons and graphic elements.

# Color Palette
A warm, sophisticated palette with depth and richness, ensuring a premium feel while maintaining a minimalist core.

*   **Primary Accent:** Rich Mocha Brown (`#6F4E37`) - Evokes coffee, warmth, and groundedness.
*   **Secondary Accent:** Warm Cream / Off-White (`#FDF8ED`) - Provides a soft, inviting background and ample white space.
*   **Text/Primary Dark:** Deep Charcoal Grey (`#2C2C2C`) - For primary text, ensuring readability and sophistication.
*   **Text/Secondary Light:** Soft Grey (`#A0A0A0`) - For secondary text, subtle details.
*   **Highlight/Tertiary:** Muted Gold / Terracotta (`#B8860B` or `#CD8A66`) - Used sparingly for highlights, CTA accents, or subtle graphic elements to add warmth and a touch of luxury.
*   **Background Neutral:** Clean White (`#FFFFFF`) - Predominant background to amplify spaciousness.

# Typography
Emphasis on legibility, modernity, and a premium feel. A clear hierarchy will be established through font size, weight, and color.

*   **Headings (H1, H2, H3):** A modern, elegant sans-serif typeface with good character spacing. Examples: *Inter*, *DM Sans*, *Cabinet Grotesk*. Focus on clean lines and strong presence.
*   **Body Text & Subheadings (H4, P):** A highly readable, clean sans-serif (could be the same as headings for consistency) or a sophisticated, minimalist serif for editorial flair. Examples: *Inter*, *IBM Plex Sans*, *New Spirit*.
*   **Call-to-Action & Navigation:** A clear, robust sans-serif that stands out without being overwhelming.
*   **Small Text/Captions:** A concise sans-serif for legibility at small sizes.

# Layout Structure

*   **Header / Navigation:**
    *   **Minimalist & Sticky:** A clean, fixed header that is compact and non-obtrusive.
    *   **Logo:** Prominently displays the "Modern Coffee House" name alongside the flat vector coffee cup icon on the left.
    *   **Navigation:** Clear, concise links centered or to the right: "Menu," "Locations," "About Us," "Contact." Possibly a subtle "Order Online" or "Reservations" button/link.
    *   **Subtle Hover:** Underline or slight color change on navigation links.
*   **Hero Section:**
    *   **Impactful Visual:** High-resolution, artistic photography of coffee (e.g., latte art, brewing process, serene interior) or a sophisticated abstract composition reflecting warmth and quality.
    *   **Bold Headline:** A concise, compelling H1 statement conveying the brand's essence and unique value proposition (e.g., "The Art of Coffee, Perfected.").
    *   **Subtle Tagline:** A short, inviting H2 or paragraph introducing the experience.
    *   **Primary CTA:** A clean, prominent button (e.g., "Explore Our Menu" or "Find a Location") with ample white space around it.
    *   **Minimal Elements:** Avoid clutter, focus on the visual and typography.
*   **Feature Sections:**
    *   **"Our Craft" / "Signature Roasts":** Utilizes a grid layout (e.g., 2 or 3 columns) with clean cards. Each card features a high-quality image, a succinct heading (H3), and a brief description, highlighting key aspects like bean origins, brewing methods, or unique roast profiles.
    *   **"The Experience":** A section showcasing the ambiance of the coffee house through a large, inviting photograph or an editorial-style layout with accompanying text about the unique atmosphere, community, or design.
    *   **"Seasonal Selections":** A dynamic carousel or grid presenting limited-time offerings, seasonal drinks, or new pastries, with clear visuals and concise details.
*   **Content Sections:**
    *   **"Our Story / Philosophy":** An editorial-style section with a strong H2 heading, ample body copy, and a supporting minimalist visual (e.g., a founder's portrait, a coffee bean illustration, or a simple abstract graphic). Focus on readability with generous line height and paragraph spacing.
    *   **"Sustainability & Sourcing":** A dedicated section with concise text and subtle icons/illustrations, emphasizing ethical practices and quality ingredient sourcing.
*   **Call-to-Action (Integrated):**
    *   **"Find Your Modern Coffee House":** A distinct section with a clear H2, a location finder input or a button leading to the locations page, potentially with a map integration preview.
    *   **Secondary CTA:** A subtle prompt for newsletter signup or social media engagement, integrated elegantly without disrupting the flow.
*   **Footer:**
    *   **Minimalist & Functional:** Dark background (e.g., Deep Charcoal Grey) with light text or clean light background.
    *   **Essential Links:** "Menu," "Locations," "About Us," "Privacy Policy," "Terms of Service."
    *   **Contact Information:** Address, phone, email (if applicable).
    *   **Social Media Icons:** Flat vector, consistent with brand style.
    *   **Copyright:** Clear and concise.

# Grid System
A robust **Swiss Grid** (also known as modular or typographic grid) will be employed, likely based on an **8-point system**, ensuring pixel-perfect alignment and consistency across all elements.
*   **Column Grid:** A flexible 12-column grid for desktop, adapting to 8 or 4 columns for tablet and mobile, respectively.
*   **Baseline Grid:** A subtle baseline grid will be maintained to ensure vertical rhythm and typographic harmony throughout the content, especially in content-heavy sections.
*   **Gutters:** Consistent gutter widths to create clear separation and hierarchy.

# Spacing System
A highly disciplined and consistent spacing system, based on multiples of an **8px base unit**, to maintain visual balance, hierarchy, and ample white space.

*   **Small (e.g., 8px):** For internal padding within components, icon-text separation.
*   **Medium (e.g., 16px):** For spacing between minor elements, list items.
*   **Large (e.g., 24px - 32px):** For spacing between paragraphs, form fields, small sections.
*   **Extra Large (e.g., 40px - 64px):** For spacing between major sections, heading-body separation.
*   **Hero Spacing (e.g., 80px - 120px+):** Generous vertical padding for hero and critical sections to create impact.

# Components

*   **Buttons:**
    *   **Primary:** Filled with a sophisticated color (e.g., Rich Mocha Brown or a muted gold), minimal border-radius (e.g., 4px), legible sans-serif text. Subtle hover effect (e.g., slight background darkening, subtle scale, or border highlight).
    *   **Secondary / Ghost:** Transparent background with a refined border (e.g., Deep Charcoal Grey or Rich Mocha Brown), clear text. Subtle hover effect to fill background or darken border.
    *   **Text Link:** Minimalist, with an elegant underline on hover.
*   **Cards:**
    *   **Clean & Elevated:** Ample internal padding, minimal or no visible border, subtle soft shadow for depth (optional, based on desired minimalism level), or a clean 1px border. Used for menu items, feature highlights, and blog previews.
    *   **Content Hierarchy:** Clear typography for title, description, and price (if applicable).
*   **Forms:**
    *   **Minimalist Input Fields:** Clean, unbordered input fields that highlight on focus with a subtle border or background change. Clear, concise labels placed above or beside inputs.
    *   **Error States:** Clearly defined but non-jarring visual cues for errors (e.g., subtle red border or text).
    *   **Submit Button:** Consistent with primary button style.
*   **Icons:**
    *   **Flat Vector Style:** Consistent line weight, monochromatic or using brand colors subtly. The coffee cup icon is central. Other icons (location, info, social media) will maintain this clean, modern style.
    *   **Simplicity:** Easily recognizable symbols.
*   **Dividers:**
    *   **Subtle:** Thin, light grey or off-white lines used sparingly to separate content sections, or rely purely on generous white space for separation. Avoid heavy or decorative dividers.

# Imagery Style
The imagery will be crucial in conveying the premium and authentic brand personality.

*   **Photography:**
    *   **Artisanal & High-Fidelity:** Focus on stunning, professional photography of coffee beans, brewing processes, latte art, freshly baked goods, and the inviting interior/exterior of the coffee house.
    *   **Composition:** Clean, minimalist compositions with depth of field, natural lighting, and a warm, inviting color grading that complements the brand palette.
    *   **Authenticity:** Real, un-staged moments that capture the essence of the experience. Avoid overly posed or generic stock photos.
*   **Illustrations:**
    *   **Flat Vector & Abstract:** Where photography isn't suitable, use flat vector illustrations. These should be minimalist, modern, and abstract, aligning with the "simple" and "flat vector" client requirement.
    *   **Conceptual:** Illustrations can be used to represent concepts like sustainability, community, or craft in a clean, visual manner.
    *   **No Cartoon/Playful:** Strictly avoid anything cartoonish or overly playful; maintain sophistication.

# Interaction & Animation
All interactions will be subtle, purposeful, and enhance the user experience without being distracting or flashy.

*   **Hover Effects:**
    *   **Buttons:** Slight background fill, subtle scale-up, or a clean border highlight.
    *   **Links:** Underline reveal, subtle text color change.
    *   **Cards/Images:** Gentle scale, slight shadow lift, or a subtle overlay to indicate interactivity.
*   **Transitions:**
    *   **Page Loads:** Smooth fade-in of elements, especially in the hero section.
    *   **Section Scrolls:** Clean, subtle transitions between sections (e.g., gentle fade-up or slide-in as they enter the viewport).
    *   **Navigation:** Smooth dropdowns or slide-ins for mobile hamburger menus.
*   **Micro-interactions:**
    *   **Form Inputs:** Subtle focus state (e.g., thin border glow or background tint).
    *   **Loading States:** Minimalist loading spinners or progress indicators.
    *   **Notifications:** Clean, non-intrusive toast messages for confirmations (e.g., "Item added to cart," "Subscription successful").

# Mobile Responsive Design
The desktop layout will be designed with a mobile-first mindset, ensuring a seamless and premium experience across all devices.

*   **Prioritize Content:** Essential information and CTAs will be prioritized and accessible.
*   **Navigation:** The minimalist header will transform into a clean, hamburger menu icon on mobile, revealing an intuitive full-screen or slide-out navigation panel.
*   **Layout Adaptation:**
    *   **Stacking:** Multi-column layouts (grids, feature sections) will gracefully stack vertically.
    *   **Image Optimization:** Images will be responsive and optimized for mobile screens without losing quality or impact.
    *   **Typography Scaling:** Font sizes will be adjusted for optimal readability on smaller screens, maintaining hierarchy.
*   **Touch Targets:** Buttons and interactive elements will have generous touch targets to prevent mis-taps.
*   **Performance:** Emphasis on fast loading times to ensure a smooth mobile experience.
*   **Maintain Premium Feel:** The overall aesthetic, spacing, and visual quality will be preserved to ensure a premium experience regardless of device.
