### **Concept 1: Serene Embrace**

**Description:** This design features a calm dog's side profile within a gentle, almost enclosing, continuous line that subtly incorporates two floating bubbles. The overall shape suggests a soft, protective embrace, reinforcing the stress-free experience. The wordmark uses a modern sans-serif with clear hierarchy.

*   **Icon:** Elegant, continuous monoline drawing of a dog's head and neck in profile, with two perfectly circular bubbles subtly integrated into the composition, creating a sense of calm and cleanliness.
*   **Composition:** Balanced, clean, and uses implied circularity for harmony.
*   **Typography:** Manrope, medium weight for "THE DOG" and regular weight for "SPA," with excellent kerning.
*   **Color Palette:** Dominant Soft Teal (#5FAFA6), Muted Sage (#A8C8B8) for bubbles, Charcoal (#2C3439) for text.
