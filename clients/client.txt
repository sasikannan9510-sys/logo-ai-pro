**Task**  
Design a minimalist initials-based logo for LJ Walker LTD.

**Context**  
Represents two distinct businesses under one brand:  
- Real Estate  
- Media / Digital Content  
Logo must not feel industry-locked. It should be timeless, premium, and versatile across platforms.

**Exemplars**  
Style inspiration: Louis Vuitton, Yves Saint Laurent, Chanel, Rolex, Cartier.  
Not copies—just the same level of simplicity, elegance, and recognition.

**Persona**  
Professional, modern, sophisticated, premium, instantly recognizable, memorable.  
Avoid generic real estate/media icons (houses, keys, cameras, microphones, clipart).

**Format**  
Deliverables: AI, EPS, SVG, PDF, PNG (transparent), JPG.  
Colors: Black, White, Charcoal, Dark Gray.  
Must scale beautifully across business cards, signage, social media, websites, embroidery, promotional products, print/digital ads, and vehicle wraps.

**Tone**  
Elegant, timeless, luxury-brand quality.  
Simple is better than complicated.  
Should still look fantastic 20 years from now.
