Create a modern, industrial, highly recognizable logo for TekMount, a manufacturer of mounting solutions for technology devices including displays, monitors, tablets, cameras, kiosks, and professional AV equipment.

The logo should communicate strength, engineering precision, innovation, reliability, and professional technical performance. It should feel clean, technical, durable, and trustworthy while remaining minimal, scalable, and suitable for industrial branding.

Use visual inspiration from:
- mounting brackets, support arms, rails, clamps, wall mounts, and hardware systems
- display mounting structures and device support solutions
- integrated T and/or M letterforms
- geometric industrial forms, modular construction, structural balance, and engineered symmetry

The logo should include:
- a strong standalone icon
- a bold professional wordmark
- clean vector construction
- excellent small-size recognition
- a timeless industrial-tech look
- suitability for embossing, laser engraving, packaging, product branding, manuals, trade show displays, vehicles, websites, and business cards

Typography:
- bold modern sans-serif
- industrial, engineered, highly legible
- clean spacing and confident proportions

Colors:
- electric blue
- charcoal gray
- black
- white
- optional metallic silver accent

Generate 5 distinct directions:
1. T/M monogram built from bracket or mount geometry
2. display-mount inspired symbol with a structural support form
3. geometric abstract icon suggesting alignment, strength, and precision
4. clean wordmark-led concept with subtle integrated technical symbolism
5. minimal industrial emblem suitable for hardware branding and engraving

Avoid:
- generic tech clipart
- overly detailed mechanical drawings
- weak or trendy startup-style logos
- cartoon graphics
- excessive gradients, chrome effects, shadows, or fake 3D
- hard-to-read typography
