Refine and professionally reproduce the **existing Jing Systems logo**.



This is **not a redesign**. The current Jing Systems logo identity must stay recognisable.



## Preserve the existing logo identity



Keep the recognisable structure and symbolism of the current logo, including:



* two stylised supportive hands forming the main outer shape

* a lotus flower in the centre

* a warm glowing light in the middle

* a calm wellness + software / booking system feel

* the existing palette direction of blue, teal, gold and cream tones



## Goal



Create a **more polished, premium, modern and professional version** of the current Jing Systems logo for a wellness software platform, while preserving the original identity and cultural feel.



## Refine the existing logo by improving



* line quality and smoothness

* hand shape clarity and symmetry

* lotus detail and petal balance

* centre glow quality and elegance

* overall visual balance and proportion

* separation between the hands, lotus and glow

* contrast and readability at small sizes

* premium SaaS / wellness brand presentation

* subtle realism and dimensional polish without changing the logo concept



## Style direction



The logo should feel:



* calm

* premium

* modern

* trustworthy

* culturally respectful

* wellness-focused

* suitable for clinic booking and management software

* elegant rather than decorative



## Important constraints



* do **not** redesign the logo

* do **not** replace the hands / lotus / glow concept

* do **not** add extra symbols

* do **not** make it look like a generic spa logo

* do **not** turn it into a religious icon

* do **not** make it cartoonish or fantasy-like

* do **not** make it look like a generic AI-generated logo



## Result



The final result should look like the **same Jing Systems logo**, but professionally redrawn and elevated to a premium brand standard suitable for website use, app use, software branding, marketing, and future animation.
