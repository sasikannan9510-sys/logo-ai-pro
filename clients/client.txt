Based on the design brief, here is a concise creative brief suitable for an AI logo generator or professional designer.

**Business Name:**
**Thermabond**

**Industry:**
Pavement Maintenance Technology • Asphalt Repair • Civil Infrastructure • Road Maintenance • Engineering Services

**Style:**

* Premium corporate wordmark
* Fortune 500 aesthetic
* Modern minimalist
* Swiss-inspired typography
* Engineering precision
* Bold, clean, geometric
* Timeless
* Professional
* Industrial elegance
* High-end B2B branding

**Requirements:**

* Design a distinctive premium wordmark or combination mark for **Thermabond**.
* Typography should carry most of the visual identity.
* Include only one subtle, ownable design element if needed.
* Convey engineering excellence, durability, precision, innovation, and trust.
* Suitable for government councils, civil contractors, infrastructure companies, and international manufacturing partners.
* Flat vector design only.
* No gradients.
* No shadows.
* No bevels.
* No 3D effects.
* No road icons.
* No asphalt rollers.
* No road cones.
* No tarmac textures.
* No generic construction symbols.
* Strong readability at favicon size.
* Must reproduce perfectly in embroidery and PPE.
* Thick, clean shapes suitable for stitching.
* Strong performance in one-color applications.
* Highly scalable from business cards to vehicle graphics.
* Memorable and trademark-friendly.

**Preferred Color Palette**

* Navy — **#1A1A2E**
* Amber — **#FFC107**
* Flame Orange — **#FF5722**
* White

**Typography**

* Modern geometric sans-serif
* Similar character to DM Sans, Neue Haas Grotesk, Avenir, or Gotham
* Custom refined letter spacing
* Strong visual balance
* Premium corporate feel

**Keywords**
Engineering • Infrastructure • Heat • Bond • Precision • Reliability • Innovation • Strength • Professional • Minimal • Modern • Corporate • Industrial • Durable • Trusted • Technical • Premium • Clean • Timeless • Distinctive

This brief is optimized for generating a logo that feels comparable in quality to leading global engineering and infrastructure brands while remaining unique and practical for real-world applications.
