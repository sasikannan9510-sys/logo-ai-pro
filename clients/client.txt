## TASK

Design a premium, vintage-inspired logo identity for a new company named "Henry's Muse."

The logo should be original, timeless, memorable, and suitable for commercial branding. It must look handcrafted by an experienced illustrator rather than AI-generated clip art.

---

## CONTEXT

Henry's Muse is a social platform that connects people who barter skills with each other to accomplish experiences and goals from their bucket lists.

The brand represents:

• Adventure
• Human connection
• Curiosity
• Generosity
• Creativity
• Shared experiences
• Friendship
• Personal growth

The logo should emotionally communicate the feeling of helping people achieve dreams together.

---

## DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

Create a vintage emblem featuring:

• Henry, a charming Miniature Schnauzer mascot
• Henry peeking out from inside an old metal bucket
• The bucket must include one subtle hole that cleverly references the phrase "bucket list."
• Henry should appear friendly, intelligent, slightly curious, and elegant.
• The bucket should have engraved vintage texture without becoming visually busy.
• Integrate the company name "Henry's Muse" naturally into the composition.
• Typography must remain readable even at favicon size.

The illustration should feel like an antique engraving, woodcut, or pen-and-ink etching.

Avoid cartoon styles.

Avoid flat geometric logos.

Avoid generic AI mascots.

---
