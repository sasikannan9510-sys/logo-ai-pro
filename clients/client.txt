# Industrial Logo & Style Guide Design – Site Fusion Equipment

## Project Overview

I’m looking for a talented and experienced brand designer to create a professional logo and comprehensive style guide for **Site Fusion Equipment**, a recently rebranded company specializing in the sale and servicing of plastic pipe welding equipment.

The company predominantly supplies self-propelled, tracked **Remedy pipe butt welders** manufactured in Italy by [Ritmo S.p.A.](https://www.ritmo.it?utm_source=chatgpt.com). The branding should visually complement the industrial design, colors, and rugged engineering aesthetic of this equipment rather than clash with it.

---

## Design Requirements

### Logo Style

* Strong industrial and engineering-inspired aesthetic
* Clean, modern, and professional
* Suitable for:

  * Machinery decals
  * Work vehicles
  * Uniforms
  * Website/social media
  * Technical documents
  * Signage

### Preferred Colors

* Predominantly:

  * Industrial orange
  * Dark charcoal / graphite
* Open to subtle supporting colors if appropriate

### Imagery & Symbol Ideas

I’m open to creative direction, including:

* Abstract industrial forms
* Pipe fusion/welding concepts
* Tracked machinery inspiration
* Heat/fusion symbolism
* Strong geometric or mechanical shapes

Avoid overly corporate, soft, or tech-startup aesthetics. The brand should feel rugged, capable, reliable, and engineered for field work.

---

## Deliverables

### Logo Package

* Primary logo
* Secondary / stacked variation
* Icon or emblem version
* Black & white versions
* Transparent background files

### Style Guide

Please include a concise but professional brand guide covering:

* Color palette (HEX/RGB/CMYK)
* Typography selections
* Logo usage rules
* Spacing and sizing guidelines
* Background applications
* Example branding applications/mockups

### File Formats

Please provide:

* AI / EPS vector files
* SVG
* PNG
* PDF brand guide

---

## Ideal Designer

The ideal candidate will have:

* Proven experience designing industrial or engineering brands
* Strong portfolio of logo and identity systems
* Understanding of machinery, construction, fabrication, or industrial sectors
* Ability to create branding that feels premium yet practical

Please include relevant portfolio examples with your proposal.

---

## Brand Personality

The brand should communicate:

* Industrial strength
* Reliability
* Precision
* Modern engineering
* Field-ready durability
* Professional service

Think: European industrial machinery meets modern Australian infrastructure contracting.

---

## Inspiration

The visual style should complement equipment similar to the tracked pipe welders produced by [Ritmo S.p.A.](https://www.ritmo.it?utm_source=chatgpt.com) — particularly their industrial orange, charcoal, and engineered mechanical aesthetic.
